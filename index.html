Maximize Your Bat's Life

Professional advice from Paray International Sports

1

Oiling

Apply 2-3 teaspoons of raw linseed oil to the face and edges. Avoid the splice and handle. Keep horizontal for 24 hours.

2

Knocking-In

Slowly compress the willow fibers with a bat mallet. Start with 4-6 hours of light-to-medium hitting before using a hard ball.

3

Storage

Store in a cool, dry place. Avoid car boots or extreme heat which can cause the willow to become brittle and crack.
